ICC World Cup 2023 Schedule is Finally Here! Get Ready to Roar

March 31, 2023 Author: admin
Share

The eagerly anticipated ICC Cricket World Cup 2023 schedule is set to take place in India, marking the 13th edition of this prestigious tournament. This time, India will host the cup solely, in the 50-over format, providing the perfect platform for teams from different nations to compete for the coveted World Cup trophy.

ICC World Cup 2023 scheduled

A total of 10 teams will be participating in this tournament, with the Cricket World Cup 2023 final scheduled for 26th November, which is sure to be an exhilarating day for cricket enthusiasts worldwide.

In order to qualify for the tournament, the top 8 teams of world cricket will receive an automatic berth, while the remaining 2 teams will have to compete in a qualification round. As a result, only the best teams will have the opportunity to participate in this event.

It’s worth mentioning that the last time India hosted the World Cup in 2011, they emerged victorious, becoming the first team to win the cup as hosts. England, the current defending champions, will undoubtedly face a tough challenge in defending their title in India, making the tournament even more exciting.

All in all, the ICC World Cup 2023 schedule promises to be a thrilling event that cricket enthusiasts won’t want to miss, as teams from around the world battle it out for the ultimate prize in cricket.
